About Me

I think it was my love of games and puzzles that got me into the field of computer science. I love the feeling of accomplishment and satisfaction when you finally work out the solution to a complex problem (especially when it comes at the cost of hours debugging).

During my years in the CS program at BYU, I have discovered that I have a passion for creating beautiful and functional designs especially on web and mobile platforms. This passion has led me to my current jobs working as a web developer for Reaction Data and the BYU McKay School of Education. At each job, I am fortunate to work with outstanding teams. Together, we get to solve all kinds of puzzles!
